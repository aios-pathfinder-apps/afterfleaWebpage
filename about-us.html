WHO WE ARE

Afterflea aiOS is like a protective tech-friend for small, local businesses in neighborhoods that could use a little help. Our main enemies? AI theft and a lack of tech know-how. Were working together with local government and non-profit groups to offer tools that help businesses connect with their customers and stay ahead in this computer-driven age.

We noticed there was a big problem when we saw how the quick move to everything digital was leaving out those who dont have lots of resources or tech knowledge. Weve decided to step up, and our first stop is San Franciscos Tenderloin neighborhood.

Just like a subscription service (think Netflix or Spotify), Afterflea aiOS offers businesses a chance to use advanced AI applications, special tools, and interfaces that are made just for them. With each subscription, businesses get access to our AI tools and resources, all for a reasonable price.

Were not just about helping businesses keep their ideas safe, though. Were also about teaching tech literacy and providing AI tools that help businesses connect with customers better. Think about things like a Local Job Finder or a Food Spot Finder in your neighborhood.

Our mission is to make sure that everyone can enjoy the benefits of technology, not just watch from the sidelines. Were working hard to protect our unique community businesses from AI theft and to bring tech tools to those who often get overlooked.

Since weve started, Afterflea aiOS has been making waves. Weve teamed up with the city of San Francisco and started testing our AI tools with businesses in the retail, food and drink, and local service industries in the Tenderloin area. The feedback has been great, with businesses reporting better interactions with customers and smoother operations. Were confident that this early success proves our approach is working, and were excited for even more businesses across the city and beyond to start using Afterflea aiOS.
